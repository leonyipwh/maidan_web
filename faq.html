Frequently Asked Questions
When will my lunch be delivered?
Delivery begins at 11:30am and all food is delivered by 12:30pm. You will receive an email as soon as your lunch is dropped off.
Where do I pick up my lunch?
MaiDan delivers to the front desk, pantry, or break room of your floor. If we are unable to access your floor, we ask that you pick up your delivery in the lobby of your building.
I cannot access my account.
It is likely that you have been logged out of your account. Please attempt to login on the top right corner of this page. For further support, please contact us at support@maidan.hk or +852 3796 5759.
How do I reset my password?
Click here. Enter and send your request, and we will email you a link to reset your password. You may also contact customer support to aid you in resetting your password.However, MaiDan employees do not have access to your password and will not be able to provide it to you.
Which browser is recommended for catering.maiDan.hk?
We recommend you use the latest versions of Chrome in order to have the best experience but we also support the following browsers:
  • Chrome (Mac, Windows, iOS, and Android)
  • Internet Explorer: Version 8 or higher
  • Safari (Mac and iOS)
Can I order after 10:30am on the day of delivery?
Unfortunately MaiDan cannot take orders after 10:30am. Restaurants begin meal preparations before receiving their orders. At 10:30am, restaurants are busy cooking and packaging orders for delivery. Adding additional orders at this time interrupts the kitchen and delays the day’s deliveries.
Will maiDan distribute each meal individually to each person that ordered?
Due to the limited amount of time to deliver all meals, maiDan is unable to unpack or monitor distribution of orders in office. Orders will be labeled so that you can conveniently recognize your order.
Will maiDan deliver lunch to my desk or another specified location?
To be considerate to all the employees of your firm, maiDan delivers to the front desk, pantry, or break room of your floor. If we are unable to access your floor, we ask that you pick up your delivery in the lobby of your building.
I want more variety in the menu or to order from a specific restaurant.
Lucky for you we are partnering with new restaurants every day. Once a new restaurant passes our catering criteria, we begin offering their meals on our menu. If you would like a specific item or restaurant added to the menu, let us know. We will do our best to get them on board.
How do you choose restaurants and menu items?
Customer feedback and popularity determine the restaurants we serve from and how often an item is available. Restaurants are also selected based on customer recommendations, Michelin listing, and service capacity.
Can I customize my order?
Unfortunately orders cannot be customized. We require our restaurants partners to provide a large number of high quality meals within a short period of time. In order to provide quality meals, a varied menu, and timely delivery, we are unable to provide order customization at this time.
Does maiDan deliver breakfast, dinner, or other times?
We will continually offer more services, but for now MaiDan only delivers lunch.
Is there delivery on public holidays?
Delivery on public holidays varies. The days we do not deliver will be reflected in the menu.
How do I signup my company for maiDan catering?
We serve several companies in Hong Kong. To enquire about our services, please contact catering@maidan.hk
